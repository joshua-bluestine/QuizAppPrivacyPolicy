Data Deletion Request

At QuizPrep, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your data from our app.

What Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Google account email and display name (used for authentication)
  • Quiz Data: User-generated quizzes, quiz progress, and mastery levels
  • Study Progress: Flagged questions and spaced repetition data
  • Subscription Status: Premium subscription information
  • Usage Data: App analytics and crash reports (anonymized)

How to Request Data Deletion

You have two options to delete your data:

Option 1: Delete Data Within the App

  1. Open the QuizPrep app
  2. Tap the profile icon in the top menu
  3. Tap "Sign Out" - this will delete all local data
  4. Your quiz progress and user-specific data will be removed from your device

Note: Cloud-synced data will be automatically removed after 90 days of inactivity.

Option 2: Request Complete Data Deletion

To request immediate and complete deletion of all your data from our servers, please contact us with the following information:

  • Your Google account email address used in the app
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request - QuizPrep"
  • Include any specific data you want deleted
Send Data Deletion Request

⚠️ Important Information

  • Processing Time: Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days
  • Irreversible: Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered
  • Account Closure: Deleting your data will close your QuizPrep account
  • Subscription: Active subscriptions will be canceled, but not refunded unless required by law
  • Legal Retention: Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as required by law

What Happens After Deletion

Once we process your deletion request:

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • All user-generated quizzes will be removed
  • Your quiz progress and mastery data will be deleted
  • Your subscription status will be cleared
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
